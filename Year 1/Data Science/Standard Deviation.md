# Standard Deviation

Standard deviation is an important measurement in the world of data science.

Formula:
$$\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N-1} \sum_{i=1}^N (x_i - \mu)^2}$$
Where:
- $x_i$ are your data points
- $\mu$ is the mean

- It measures how spread out the values in your dataset are around the mean.
- It is useful for [[Uncertainty in Data|uncertainty]]:
	- A large standard deviation = values are more spread out = more uncertainty
	- A small standard deviation = values are less spread out = less uncertainty
