# Binary Relations

A binary relation is a general concept, it involves pairs of elements from one or two sets.

When looking at relations, rather than saying $R$ is a relation $S \rightarrow S$ you could just say $R$ is a binary relation on $S$.
Usually the 'binary' is dropped.

For a small finite Set $S$ it is possible to draw a graph to visualise it.
