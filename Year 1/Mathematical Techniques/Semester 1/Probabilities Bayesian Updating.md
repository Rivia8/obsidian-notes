# Bayesian Updating

Bayesian updating is a method used to update the probability of a hypothesis as more evidence or information becomes available. 
It is based on Bayes' Theorem, which provides a mathematical way to revise existing belief's in light of new data.

Formula:
$$P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H) \cdot P(H) }{P(E)} $$
- $P(H|E)$ : The probability of $H$ knowing that $E$ has happened,
- $P(E|H)$ : The probability of the evidence $E$ happening given that $H$ is true,
- $P(H)$ : The initial probability of $H$ before any evidence,
- $P(E)$ : The total probability of the evidence $E$
