	
According to current research, the most scientifically accurate formula for calculating maintenance calories is the Mifflin-St Jeor equation. This formula takes into account factors like weight, height, age, and sex to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is then multiplied by an activity level factor to determine your maintenance calorie needs. 

- Males: BMR = (10 x weight in kg) + (6.25 x height in cm) - (5 x age in years) + 5
- Females: BMR = (10 x weight in kg) + (6.25 x height in cm) - (5 x age in years) - 161
