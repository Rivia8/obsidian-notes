# SQL VS NoSQL

The fundamental difference between [[SQL]] and [[NoSQL]] lies in their data structure and approach to storing information:
**SQL databases are relational,** organising data into structured tables with predefined schemas, much like a well-organised spreadsheet, whereas **NOSQL databases are non-relational**, offering more flexibility by storing data in various formats without a fixed structure.
