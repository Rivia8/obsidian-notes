# Testing

**VSLI Testing (Sometimes called Validation):** Testing is done at *silicone level* to validate the quality of silicon. But found at validation level could be fixed only be the recycle of silicon which is an extremely costly process.

Testing is what is as follows:

- verification - have you made what you have intended,
- validation - does your design fulfil the requirements
- testing - does this *particular part* work

The first two apply to the software aspect and the last is solely hardware.
