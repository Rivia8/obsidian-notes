# ERD To Schema

Once we have created our ERD, which is in the PDM form, we can then produce the **Schema.**

The [[Physical Data Model]] is the final **detailed** blueprint where from there then produce the schema. There are some guidelines to follow for this process:

- entity: each entity type becomes a relation (table)
- attribute. entity attributes become attributes of the relation
- the identifier (*) of the entity becomes the PK of the relation
- a relationship is expressed as an FK / joining table
