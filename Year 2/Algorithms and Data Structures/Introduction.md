# Introduction

- 50:50 Coursework : Exam split

Each week will have:
- Videos explaining content,
- Directed reading, curated high-quality material with pointers,
- Formative quizzes,
- Some formative and some summative worksheet / lab based coursework

Pretty much every week you need to watch the videos, do the Lab/Sheet work and read the reading material.

For the reading there are two textbooks used:
1. **Algorithm Design  and Applications by Goodrich and Tamassia.**
2. **Introduction to Algorithms by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, and Stein** *(Referred to as the MIT algorithms books)*
